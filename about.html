about

I'm Josh. I am a front-end web developer based in Vancouver.

My passion for web development started when I took a computer science introduction course at SFU and wrote my first Hello World program. Since then, I started reading about the latest news and trends about programming and web development. Although my major was in Economics, I think it shares many similarities with programming in general. Analyzing, reasoning, and identifying the essence of a problem are skills that can help me in any fields. After graduating with a Bachelor of Arts in Economics from SFU in 2017, I decided to take my passion a step further and applied for the Technical Web Design program at BCIT, which I completed in the summer of 2018. My focus is on front-end web development but I am always willing to learn new technologies and skills that are needed.

As a fresh bilingual graduate who is passionate about web development and is always striving for continous improvements, whether it is translating wireframes to actual code, validating styles and interactivity among different devices, or implemeting SEO strategies, I love it. I have experience building custom websites that fits the needs of the client and is always looking for new challenges to solve. I aim to become a top web developer and reach a position where I can assist others in making their visions to reality.

I grew up in Canada, but I was born in Taiwan. I spent some of my high school years in a small Vancouver Island city called Nanaimo before moving to Vancouver. I speak fluent Mandarin and can read and write Chinese. Check out my work!

Thanks for stopping by!
