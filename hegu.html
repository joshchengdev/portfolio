Hegu International

Hegu International is a subsidary company of BizField Angel Network that specializes in providing investment services specific for Chinese angel investor. I worked in collaboration with UI designer to create a custom Chinese WordPress website. Reporting to the CEO and the marketing director, my responsibilites were to transform mockups from PSD files to WordPress.

The Process

After discussing with the CEO and marketing director on the details of the website, the UI designer and I sat down together, discussed the project, and made mockups of every page. We had a waterfall workflow where we tried to finish one task at a time before moving on to the next task.
